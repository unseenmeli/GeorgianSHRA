Membership Options

Standard Membership

• Eligibility: Open to all individuals with a higher medical education, including doctors of any specialty, medical professors, students, residents, doctoral students, and nurses.

Benefits:

• Regular updates on reproductive and sexual health news via email.

• Access to monitoring the association's activities.

• Direct access to the association's guidelines, protocols, and patient information.

• Notifications about events organized by the association.

Affiliate Membership

• Eligibility: Same as standard membership.

Benefits:

• Includes all benefits of standard membership.

• Opportunities to participate actively in association activities, such as translating medical literature, organizing events, etc.

• Guaranteed attendance at events with a 20% discount on paid events.

Honorary Membership

• Eligibility: Awarded to individuals who have made significant contributions to the field and are nominated by existing members.

Benefits:

• Includes all benefits of affiliate membership.

• Opportunity to initiate and implement personal projects within the association.

• Guaranteed attendance/participation at all association events with a 50% discount on paid events.

• Prominent recognition on the association’s website.

Remote Friendly Membership

• Eligibility: Available to individuals outside the medical field who have an interest in reproductive and sexual health education.

Benefits:

• Receives updates on the latest advancements in reproductive and sexual health via email.

• Access to monitor the activities of the association.

Membership Fees

• All memberships are free until 2025, except for Honorary Membership.

• Honorary Membership Fee: 200 GEL per year until 2025.
