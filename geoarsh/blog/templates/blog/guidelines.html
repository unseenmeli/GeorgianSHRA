The Georgian Academy of Pediatric and Adolescent Gynecology (GAPAG)

The Georgian Academy of Pediatric and Adolescent Gynecology (GAPAG) operates as a division of the Georgian Association of Reproductive and Sexual Health (GARSH).

Our mission is to advance education, scientific research, and clinical excellence in the care of children and adolescents, with a particular focus on reproductive and sexual health. Through collaboration, training, and innovation, the Academy strives to improve standards of care and promote the well-being of young people across Georgia.
