The Georgian Association of Reproductive and Sexual Health held a Continuing Medical Education (CME) training on July 4-5

October 13, 2025 News

The Georgian Association of Reproductive and Sexual Health held a Continuing Medical Education (CME) training on July 4-5, 2025, on the topic: "Modern Principles for the Management of Estrogen Deficiency Conditions." Participants received 12 credit points. Training Speakers: Prof. Jenaro Kristashvili Prof. Elene Asanidze Prof. Manana Urjumelashvili The training was intended for certified physicians in the following specialties: 👩‍⚕️ Obstetrics and Gynecology 👩‍⚕️ Reproductive Medicine 👩‍⚕️ Family Medicine
