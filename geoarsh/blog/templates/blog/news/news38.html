📣 The Georgian Association of Reproductive and Sexual Health conducted a Continuing Medical Education (CME) training on the topic

October 10-11, 2025 News


📣 The Georgian Association of Reproductive and Sexual Health
conducted a Continuing Medical Education (CME) training on the topic:
🩺 "Modern Principles of Management of Estrogen Deficiency Conditions"
📅 Octomber 10-11, 2025
Training Speakers:
🧑‍🎓 Prof. Jenaro Kristesashvili
🧑‍🎓 Prof. Elene Asanidze
🧑‍🎓 Prof. Manana Urjumelashvili
The training was designed for certified specialists in:
👩‍⚕️ Obstetrics and Gynecology
👩‍⚕️ Reproductive Medicine
👩‍⚕️ Family Medicine
The event took place at The Zhordania Medical Center, where participants were welcomed in an academic and friendly atmosphere. A heartfelt thank you to all attendees for their great interest and active participation in discussions! 💬
📅 Our next training will be held on November 14–15, 2025.
📞 For registration and additional information, please contact us at 599 109 195.

📣 საქართველოს რეპროდუქციული და სქესობრივი ჯანმრთელობის ასოციაციამ 2025 წლის 10-11 ოქტომბერს ჩაატარა უწყვეტი სამედიცინო განათლების ტრენინგი თემაზე: "ესტროგენდეფიციტური მდგომარეობების მართვის თანამედროვე პრინციპები".

🧑‍🎓პროფ. ჯენარო ქრისტესაშვილი
