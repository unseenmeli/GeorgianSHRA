The Georgian Association of Reproductive and Sexual Health congratulates the Association's co-founder and chief expert, Prof. Jenaro Kristesashvili, on being awarded the title of Honorary Citizen of Tbilisi!

In response to the question of what this title means to him, he said:

For me, as a Tbilisian of several generations, there is probably no title more important or more emotional than being an Honorary Citizen of Tbilisi.
My childhood, adulthood, professional development, and personal formation have always been closely connected to Tbilisi. It is this city that taught me core values and instilled in me a love for my surroundings, for people, and for my work.
For me, Tbilisi is one of the most beautiful citiesβ€”not only for its appearance, architecture, and nature, but, above all, for the people who live here. Here live my family members, relatives, friends, loved ones, and complete strangersβ€”yet true Tbilisiansβ€”distinguished by tolerance, sincerity, mutual respect, and a deep love for their city.
Throughout my life, despite many attractive opportunities, I have never considered leaving Tbilisi, because I believe my knowledge and experience should be shared with the people living in my city and country, and that I should help nurture new generations of doctors and scientists.
The title of Honorary Citizen of Tbilisi is a great honor and, at the same time, a responsibility. My greatest wish is for Tbilisi to continually develop and grow stronger without losing what truly makes it specialβ€”warmth, love, and mutual respect.
My deepest thanks to everyone who deemed me worthy of this honor, and to all who congratulated me and shared in this joy.
