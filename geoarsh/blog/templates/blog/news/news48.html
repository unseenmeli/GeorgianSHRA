🇬🇪🌍 The Georgian Association of Reproductive and Sexual Health (GARSH) participated in the 40th Anniversary International Congress of the International Society of Gynecological Endocrinology (ISGE), held in Rome on March 4–6, 2025.

May 09, 2026 News

🇬🇪🌍 The Georgian Association of Reproductive and Sexual Health (GARSH) participated in the 40th Anniversary International Congress of the International Society of Gynecological Endocrinology (ISGE), held in Rome on March 4–6, 2025.
🌍The congress brought together leading specialists in reproductive medicine and gynecological endocrinology from many countries around the world. The meeting addressed current challenges in women’s reproductive health, recent scientific advances, and key issues in clinical practice.
For GARSH, it was a great honor to participate in this large-scale and historic scientific forum.
🧑‍🎓The President of the Association, Professor Elene Asanidze, was invited to deliver an expert lecture, once again highlighting the international recognition of Georgian specialists and the importance of Georgia’s representation in the global scientific community.
GARSH scientific activities at the congress:
🧑‍🎓Symposium – Endocrine Disorders and Women’s Health
🔹 Prof. Elene Asanidze
Complete Androgen Insensitivity Syndrome: Complexities in Diagnosis, Management, and Long-Term Follow-Up
🧑‍🎓Oral Presentations:
🔹 Prof. Ludmila Barbakadze (TSU)
"Different Timing, Same Results: Ovulation Induction Strategies in Fertility Preservation".
🔹 Aleksandre Asanidze (TSMU-student)
"Recurrent Pregnancy Loss in PCOS: The Role of Hyperhomocysteinemia and Insulin Resistance
🧑‍🎓Poster Presentations".
🔹Prof. Manana Gegechkori, Prof. Nani Marsagishvili
Unicornuate Uterus with Rudimentary Horn – Prospective Gynecological and Obstetric Complications
🔹 Nino Kupreishvili, Natia Gigolashvili.
"The Potential Impact of Protein Supplements on Uterine Fibroid Growth: A Clinical Case Report".
🧑‍🎓Session Chairing
🔹 Gynecological and Obstetrical Endocrinology
Chairpersons: Eleni Armeni, Elene Asanidze
🔹 Pregnancy
Chairpersons: Lorenza Driul, Aleksandre Asanidze
It is of special importance to us to represent Georgia with dignity in the international scientific arena, to share professional experience, and to further strengthen international collaboration.
