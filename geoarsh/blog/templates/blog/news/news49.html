Organized by the Georgian Association of Reproductive and Sexual Health, the following training was successfully held on March 20–21, 2026:
📘 “Modern Approaches to the Detection and Management of Reproductive Health Disorders in Children and Adolescentss”
The training was led by:
🧑‍🎓👩‍⚕️ Prof. Jenaro Kristesashvili
🧑‍🎓👩‍⚕️ Prof. Manana Maka Gegechkori
🧑‍🎓👩‍⚕️ Prof. Elene Asanidze
The training was conducted for certified physicians in the following specialties:
Obstetrics and Gynecology, Reproductive Medicine, Pediatrics, Endocrinology, and Family Medicine.
🎓 Participants were awarded 12 CME credit points.
💬 We sincerely thank all participants for their great interest, active engagement, and inspiring discussions.
